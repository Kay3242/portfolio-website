This stylized flower was modeled and composed entirely in Blender, focusing on form, symmetry, and organic curvature. Inspired by the elegance of spider lilies, the piece emphasizes delicacy through flowing petals and extended filaments. Rendered in a soft grayscale, the work invites attention to structure and silhouette over surface detail, showcasing control of shape language and composition.

This render presents a stylized pair of spider lilies preserved under a glass dome, symbolizing beauty suspended in time. The fallen petals suggest quiet decay, inviting reflection on memory, impermanence, and containment.

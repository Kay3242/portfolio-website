About Me

“I design with clarity, craft and
curiosity.”

I’m a designer with a background in both Graphic Design and User Experience Design, currently completing my undergraduate studies in the Bachelor of Design at the University of Melbourne.

My academic journey has shaped a practice grounded in both visual craft and user-centered thinking — from typography and layout systems to interface design and accessibility. I approach each project with a focus on clarity, detail, and thoughtful experimentation. Whether it’s branding, editorial design, or UX prototyping, I aim to create work that communicates with purpose.

I study at the Victorian College of the Arts (VCA) as part of my Graphic Design major, where I’ve explored themes of branding, video editing, animation, and 3D modelling.

This vibrant event poster invites students to participate in a hands-on tote bag painting session hosted by the Graphic Design Club. The playful phrase “Tote-ally made by me” highlights the personal, DIY nature of the workshop, while the soft pink backdrop and scattered paintbrushes create an inviting, creative atmosphere. With two session times and snacks provided, the design effectively communicates all essential details while maintaining a fun and approachable tone.

Optimized for social media and website banners, this layout condenses both the branding and time details into a compact, eye-catching horizontal format. It serves as a versatile asset for online promotion, maintaining consistency with the event’s visual identity.

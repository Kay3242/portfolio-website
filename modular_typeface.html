This modular typeface explores the interplay of curved and rectangular forms within a strict square grid, creating a sense of rhythm and visual density. Circular cutouts and vertical strokes introduce a playful architectural tension, resulting in a cohesive yet quirky letterset. The uniform weight and interlocking modules give the lowercase alphabet a sculptural, almost monolithic presence.

The numerical set continues the modular logic with bold, geometric silhouettes and carefully considered cutouts. Rounded counters contrast with sharp corners to maintain legibility while preserving the typeface's visual identity. Each number feels both industrial and expressive, pushing the limits of modular construction without sacrificing clarity.

This large-scale billboard showcases the modular typeface in a bold, high-contrast music promotion. The custom lettering of “pulse” emphasizes rhythm and flow, echoing the theme of sound and energy. By integrating the typeface with vibrant yellow accents and dynamic photography, the design demonstrates how the type can hold its own in real-world branding and advertising contexts.

This poster series explores emotional dualities through typographic expression, blending phrases like “bloom” and “cry me a river” with bold, modular lettering. The typeface’s rigid grid and softened curves contrast the raw human imagery and vibrant graphic overlays, amplifying both tension and intimacy. It demonstrates how type can function not only as text but as a visual voice within contemporary emotional storytelling.

This poster explores the theme of identity distortion through layered visuals, glitch effects, and typographic tension. The modular typeface adds a sense of digital structure that contrasts with the emotional fluidity of the image, emphasizing fragmentation and control. Vertical alignment and pixelated overlays push the type’s grid system into a new, expressive territory.

This framed poster blends typographic experimentation with a warm, nostalgic tone, using the modular typeface to spell “sunny” in a bold, circular lockup. The rounded, rhythmic shapes of the type echo the sun motif while contrasting the grayscale figure below. Through minimalism and metaphor, the piece captures a personal resolution wrapped in optimism and design clarity.

This playful digital piece applies the modular typeface to a whimsical cat-themed layout, balancing typographic rigidity with illustrated charm. The contrast between the type’s bold, geometric forms and the soft, curved cat illustrations creates a harmonious tension that’s both structured and inviting. It highlights the typeface’s versatility in lighthearted, contemporary branding or UI contexts.

This poster was created to promote the exhibition showcasing my artist books. It visually echoes the themes of space, memory, and fragmentation using layered textures, deconstructed typography, and papercraft elements. The goal was to communicate the tactile and emotional essence of the book within a single, impactful visual frame.

The poster was developed in consultation with Amanda Morgan, the subject coordinator, who provided detailed feedback on layout and color adjustments. Her input helped refine the visual tone and align the poster with the broader exhibition aesthetic. This collaboration ensured clarity in messaging while preserving the conceptual depth of the original book project.

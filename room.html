This low poly interior scene was modeled in Blender with a focus on simple geometry and playful proportions. I used subdivision-friendly topology and non-destructive modifiers to build modular furniture pieces like the bed, lamp, shelf, and windows, achieving a clean, stylized result suited for animation or game assets.

After modeling, I applied flat color materials with no textures to maintain the charm of the low poly aesthetic. I lit the scene with soft area lights and an HDRI to give depth while preserving a toy-like atmosphere. Final renders were done in Cycles to highlight the subtle shadows and clean color transitions.
